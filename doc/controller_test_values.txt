Tested on a Logitech F310

Joystick axes:

Axis 0: Left stick, left (-1) to right (1)
Axis 1: Left stick, top (-1) to bottom (1)
Axis 2: Left trigger, uncompressed (-1) to compressed (1)
Axis 3: Right stick, left (-1) to right (1)
Axis 4: Right stick, top (-1) to bottom (1)
Axis 5: Right trigger, uncompressed (-1) to compressed (1)

Note: triggers do not produce values until initially compressed. Sticks center slightly off a 0 value on the axis

Button values:

A: 0
B: 1
X: 2
Y: 3
Left bumper: 4
Right bumper: 5
Select: 6
Start: 7
